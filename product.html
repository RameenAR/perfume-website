How to Wear It

🌡️

Pulse Points

Apply to wrists, neck, behind ears, and inner elbows. These warm spots amplify the fragrance and help it project naturally.

Moisturised Skin

Fragrance lasts longer on moisturised skin. Apply an unscented lotion before spraying for maximum longevity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this fragrance long-lasting?

All Scent Luxe fragrances are Eau de Parfum concentration (20–30% aromatic compounds), designed for exceptional longevity of 8–12+ hours on skin. Longevity varies based on skin type, humidity, and application method.

Are your perfumes cruelty-free?

Absolutely. Every Scent Luxe fragrance is 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We are certified by PETA and Leaping Bunny. Our ingredients are ethically and sustainably sourced.

Do you offer samples before buying?

Yes! We offer a Discovery Set of 6 × 2ml samples for $25 (credited toward your first full bottle purchase). Contact us to request your personalised sample selection based on your preferences.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely in love with your purchase, return it in its original packaging for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked.

How should I store my perfume?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid bathrooms (humidity degrades fragrance). Keep the bottle upright and tightly capped. Properly stored, a fragrance lasts 3–5 years.
