Where It Began

A Passion for the Art of Scent

Scent Luxe was born from a single obsession: the belief that a great fragrance is not merely a product — it is a living artwork. Founded in 2010 by master perfumer Isabelle Moreau in the lavender-scented gardens of Grasse, France, our house was built on the radical idea that luxury should be accessible without compromise.

Every perfume in our collection begins its journey in the field — sourced from ethical, sustainable farms across the world's most fragrant regions. From Damascene rose fields in Bulgaria to Madagascan vanilla plantations, from Mysore sandalwood forests to the agarwood groves of Assam, we trace every ingredient to its origin.

Today, Scent Luxe curates over 12 exclusive fragrances, each collaboratively crafted with some of the world's most celebrated noses. We believe scent is the most intimate form of self-expression — and we are honoured to be part of your story.

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Isabelle Moreau launches Scent Luxe in Grasse with just three fragrances and a vision: to make master perfumery accessible to everyone who appreciates beauty.

2013

The First Award

Midnight Rose wins "Fragrance of the Year" at the International Perfume Awards — our first of six consecutive awards, cementing our reputation for excellence.

2016

Going Global

We open our first flagship store in Paris and begin shipping worldwide. Within six months, we're delivering to 25 countries across 5 continents.

2019

Sustainability Pledge

We achieve PETA cruelty-free certification and launch our ethical sourcing programme — partnering directly with 18 farmers in 9 countries for full supply chain transparency.

2022

50,000 Customers

We celebrate reaching 50,000 happy customers worldwide. Golden Oud — our most complex creation — becomes our fastest-selling fragrance ever.

2026

Spring Collection

Launch of the Spring 2026 Collection — four new fragrances that push the boundaries of what is possible in artisan perfumery. The journey continues.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every bottle we create and every decision we make

🎨
Artisan Craftsmanship

Every fragrance is hand-crafted in small batches by master perfumers with decades of experience. No shortcuts. No compromise. No mass production.

🌍
Sustainable Sourcing

We partner exclusively with ethical farms and co-operatives. Our ingredients are traceable, fairly traded, and ecologically responsible in every country we source from.

✨
Timeless Elegance

We reject trend-driven perfumery. Every Scent Luxe fragrance is designed to transcend seasons and endure as a personal signature for years, even decades.

🤝
Honest Transparency

Full ingredient disclosure, fair pricing, and honest communication. We believe you deserve to know exactly what you are putting on your skin and why.

💡
Continuous Innovation

We never stop learning. Our perfumers spend months experimenting for each new fragrance, collaborating with scientists and botanists to push what is possible.

❤️
Community First

From the farmers who grow our ingredients to the customers who wear our fragrances — every person in our supply chain is treated with dignity and care.

From Field to Bottle

Every Scent Luxe fragrance takes 18–24 months from concept to launch

01
🌸
Inspiration

Our perfumers draw inspiration from travel, nature, art, and memory. Each fragrance begins as an emotion — a place, a moment, a feeling.

02
🌿
Sourcing

We travel to source the finest raw materials — meeting farmers face-to-face and selecting only the highest quality harvest from ethical partners.

03
⚗️
Formulation

Hundreds of trial formulations. Our perfumers blend, test, and refine — sometimes over 200 iterations before a formula is deemed worthy of the Scent Luxe name.

04
👃
Evaluation

Every formula is evaluated on skin — not strip paper — over multiple days, in multiple climates, worn by real people with real lives.

05
🏺
Bottling

Each bottle is hand-filled, inspected, and sealed. Our signature packaging is assembled by a small team of artisans in our Grasse studio.

06
✦
Delivery

Your fragrance is shipped in a bespoke Scent Luxe gift box with gold ribbon, tissue paper, and a handwritten card — because presentation matters.
